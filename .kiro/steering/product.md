# Product Overview

GrowBro is a mobile cultivation tracking app for home cannabis growers. The app helps users plan, track, and optimize their growing operations through educational tools and data-driven insights.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Educational Focus**: Provides cultivation knowledge and best practices without selling products
- **Privacy-First**: Explicit opt-ins for data sharing, respects user privacy
- **Offline-First**: Core features work without internet connectivity
- **Age-Gated**: 18+ with legal disclaimers and compliance measures

## Key Features

- **Calendar & Tasks**: Recurring task management, reminders, and agenda planning
- **Plant Tracking**: Individual plant lifecycle management from seed to harvest
- **Strain Database**: Browse and favorite strains with detailed grow information
- **Nutrient Engine**: Feeding schedules and nutrient calculations
- **Harvest â†’ Inventory**: Drying, curing, and inventory tracking workflows
- **AI Photo Assessment**: Plant health analysis via trichome and issue detection
- **Guided Playbooks**: Step-by-step cultivation guides for different scenarios
- **Community Feed**: Social sharing with moderation system

## Business Model

Subscription-based monetization. No product sales within the app.

## Compliance Requirements

- App store policy compliance (iOS and Android)
- Regional legal regulations for cannabis-related content
- Age verification and legal disclaimers
- Content moderation for community features
